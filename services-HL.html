Overview

The Electronic Jacquard HL Series leads textile automation with advanced electronics and traditional jacquard precision. Featuring next-generation programmable logic, an intuitive control interface, and real-time feedback, it enables seamless pattern transitions, customization, and consistent quality. Designed for demanding environments, the HL Series boosts productivity and streamlines both creative and manufacturing processes for small-scale operations and large industries alike.

Key Benifits

The HL Series is engineered for high-level performance and precision, blending advanced electronics with classic jacquard weaving techniques to handle even the most intricate fabric designs seamlessly.
